Setting Portfolio Parameters

There are four required settings; (1) The total amount invested is used to compute the dollars invested in each security of the resulting portfolio and in the determination of liquidity limits (see below) constraining the optimization. (2) The minimum acceptable rate and (3) the benchmark return rate are both used in the computation of summary and descriptive statistics for individual securities and portfolios. (4) The reference index ETF is used as the proxy for the market throughout. Select your preferred ETF from the drop-down box.
