Setting Upper Bounds on Allocations

Upper and lower bounds may be set manually and/or with the assistance of a simple dialog.

The upper bound is the maximum percentage of the initial total portfolio allocated to a security. The sum of the upper bounds cannot sum to less than 100 % (to do so means the available funds are not being fully invested – required condition for optimization).

The lower bound percentage forces the optimization search to allocate at least that percentage of the total to the chosen security. The sum of the lower bounds cannot exceed 100% (tom do so means the allocation will exceed the available funds -a violation of optimization).
